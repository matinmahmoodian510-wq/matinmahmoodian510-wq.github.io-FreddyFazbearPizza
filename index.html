ABOUT FREDDY FAZBEAR'S PIZZA

Our original location in Hurricane, Utah
The fun and friendly atmosphere inside our restaurant
Our famous animatronic band: Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy

Freddy Fazbear's Pizza started its operations in 1983 and has become one of the most popular chain restaurants by providing delicious pizzas and a fun environment for children and families.

Our first location was established in Hurricane, Utah, USA, and we have since expanded to multiple locations across the United States and internationally.

We use the freshest ingredients and unique recipes to prepare pizzas with an unparalleled taste that is delicious and enjoyable for all ages.
